Worlds Best Honey Garlic Pork Chops

The very best honey garlic pork chops ever to be made

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 2/3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 6 pork chops

Steps

  1. preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate
  2. whisk the ketchup, honey, soy sauce and garlic together in a bowl and make a glaze
  3. sear the pork chops on both sides, lightly brush the glaze on each side, grill until no longer pink around 9 mins
