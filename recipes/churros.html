Churros

Easy to make churro recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 quarts oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground sugar

Steps

  1. Combine water, sugar, salt and vegetable oil in a saucepan and place over medium heat bring to boil and stir in flour
  2. heat oil in frying pan or deep fryer
  3. transfer dough to bag with star tip on and pipe 6 inch strips into the oil cook until golden and remove with slotted spoon
  4. combine the remaining sugar and cinnamon and roll churros in mixture
