Classic old fashioned

A classic recipe for a classic drink

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons simple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 shot bourbon whisky
  • 1 slice orange
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Steps

  1. pour the simple syrup, water, and bitters into a whisky glass and stir
  2. Add the ice in glass and pour the bourbon over
  3. top with orange slice and maraschino cherry
